Hello

We are Voyage

Someone said once, “To Travel is to Live”, why is it then that 8 out of 10 people suffer from pre-holiday stress. From booking flights and accommodation to choosing activities to do, when did holidaying become more about schedules and lists than about adventure? What if there was a way to reduce this stress and make holiday planning fun again? Introducing Voyage, a web and phone app that brings everything into one place. Want to book flights? Voyage has the best deals. Accommodation? Voyage will ensure you get a great nights sleep. Want to find a hidden gem? Voyage will take you on the path least travelled!

About Voyage

The app that will take your travel to the next level

Travel Planner

Whether it’s your first time travelling or you’re a seasoned pro, everyone forgets. Luckily Voyage is here to save the day. It has all the steps you need pre-programmed in, you can even add and remove steps as you see fit or even start completely from scratch. Pre-programmed Plans tell you everything you need to know before, during and after your trip

Itinerary

First, and foremost Voyage is a way to keep organised during your vacation. And the best way to stay organised is with an itinerary! Voyage will link with your email and sync ticket and booking information storing it all in one place! You can choose to display this information in a variety of formats including by day, week or month, in a list or calendar. You can even allow other people you are travelling with to collaborate with you.

Hidden Gems Map

Maybe you don’t want to have such a rigid schedule, maybe you do want to walk the path less travelled. Well with Voyage you can. Whether you’re looking to taste authentic cuisine, or you want to take in the fresh air of a secluded outdoor location, Voyage will tailor the Hidden Gems map to you. Simply sync your Travellers Profile to the app and voila! You’ve got a personalised tour of your location. Simply follow the road less travels, or chose a specific item to add to your itinerary.

Traveller Profile & Journal

Once you get the itch to go travelling, you may never want to stop! That's why we’ve created your Travel Profile and Journal, so you can keep track of it all along the way. Tell us all about you in your Travel Profile and we’ll tailor your app experience to you! In your Journal, you can document those great experiences adding photos, stickers and audio! When you’re ready simply download your Journal or even get it printed!
